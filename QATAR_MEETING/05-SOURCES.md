## Evidence Notes

- MyHealth supports secure patient access to parts of the electronic medical record across HMC and
  PHCC.
- HMC has public material describing Oracle Cerner EHR/CIS usage and integration with Qatar Biobank.
- Nar'aakom supports PHCC service workflows such as appointments, dependents, health-card-related
  services, health center/family physician services, and video consultation.
- Qatar's 2024-2030 health strategy direction emphasizes population health, patient-care excellence,
  efficiency, resilience, integration, sustainability, and innovation.
- TASMU positions healthcare as a smart-sector priority and names stakeholders across government,
  providers, insurers, pharmacies, research, and citizens.
- Qatar's privacy and AI direction make consent, audit, human control, and sovereign AI governance
  central to the SANAD answer.
