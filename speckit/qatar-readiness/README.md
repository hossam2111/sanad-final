## Golden Answer

Qatar does not need a replacement for its existing digital health systems. Qatar already has strong
systems of record and service channels. SANAD adds the governed intelligence layer above them:
explainable risk, consent, role-based workflows, audit, and sovereign AI control.

## Meeting Posture

- Respect Qatar's digital maturity first.
- Position SANAD as a layer above existing systems, not a competing portal or EMR.
- Tie the answer to Qatar's 2024-2030 health priorities: integrated care, efficiency, resilience,
  innovation, and population health.
- Avoid exact savings claims until a local discovery/pilot study.
- Do not say Qatar production deployment is complete; say the platform/reference demo is ready and
  production needs integration, hosting, legal, and clinical governance work.
