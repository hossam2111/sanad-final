# Qatar Capability Map

## Existing Qatar Assets vs SANAD

| Existing capability | What it does well | What SANAD adds |
|---|---|---|
| Oracle Cerner / CIS / EMR | Clinical documentation, orders, results, structured patient data | Cross-system intelligence, explainable risk, AI governance, role-specific decision workflows, national command view |
| MyHealth | Patient access to labs, medications, appointments, diagnoses, immunizations, and visit summaries | Consent enforcement, family/insurance/emergency workflows, audit explanation, AI health-plan support |
| Nar'aakom | PHCC service access, appointments, health card workflows, dependents, family physician/health center services, video consultation | Decision workflows beyond service requests, cross-role orchestration, consent-aware care journeys |
| TASMU Smart Qatar | Smart-sector framework, ecosystem and KPI orientation | Concrete operating layer that can connect stakeholders and measure outcomes |
| Qatar National Health Strategy 2024-2030 | Population health, excellence in patient care, efficiency, resilience, integration, innovation | Productized execution layer for integrated care, governance, AI control, and command visibility |
| HMC Healthcare Strategy 2024-2030 | Multi-provider integration, access, quality, precision medicine, emerging digital technology | Operational bridge between strategy and daily decisions |
| Privacy law and AI strategy direction | Policy foundation for data protection and AI adoption | Product enforcement: consent, role access, audit evidence, human override, sovereign model/key control |

## SANAD Layer Definition

SANAD sits above systems of record and service channels.

It should be described as:

- Decision-intelligence layer.
- Consent and trust layer.
- Audit and accountability layer.
- Cross-role workflow layer.
- Sovereign AI governance layer.
- National command layer.

It should not be described as:

- A replacement EMR.
- A replacement patient portal.
- A replacement appointment app.
- A completed Qatar production deployment.
- A Qatar-only product.

## Stakeholder Value

| Stakeholder | Value message |
|---|---|
| Doctor | Prioritized risk, explanation, medication safety, human override |
| Patient / citizen | Transparent access, consent, trust, understandable health plan |
| Family | Governed access instead of informal sharing |
| Ministry / government | National visibility, governance, audit, AI control |
| Hospital leadership | Capacity/risk view, escalation, workflow discipline |
| Insurance / payer | Consent-gated workflows and explainable review boundaries |
| Investor / board | Scalable sovereign health-intelligence platform, not a one-country demo |

## Success Metrics For A Qatar Pilot

Use these as discovery/pilot targets, not as claimed achieved numbers:

- Time from risk signal to reviewed action.
- Percentage of AI recommendations with clinician confirmation or override reason.
- Consent request completion/revocation rate.
- Access events with complete audit evidence.
- Reduction in manual coordination steps for selected workflows.
- Leadership dashboard refresh and incident-review cadence.
- User satisfaction by doctor, patient/citizen, and admin roles.
