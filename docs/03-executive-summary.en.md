## The Problem

Healthcare decisions across the Kingdom are made on fragmented data, with limited traceability and no unified accountability framework. Three structural gaps persist:

1. **Fragmented clinical intelligence.** Prescribing, dispensing, laboratory, and emergency data reside in disconnected systems. A drug interaction that should be caught at the point of care is invisible because the systems do not communicate.

2. **Untraceable AI.** As AI enters clinical workflows, no mechanism exists to answer: who triggered a decision, what data informed it, when it was made, and why that conclusion — and to verify that the record has not been altered.

3. **Sovereignty exposure.** Reliance on foreign health-AI platforms creates dependencies on external data processing, foreign regulatory frameworks, and opaque algorithmic governance that conflict with PDPL obligations and national interests.

---

## The Solution: SANAD Health

SANAD Health is a national AI health-intelligence platform built on a single architectural principle: **every decision must be traceable, explainable, and sovereign**.

The platform is developed by **Sanad Technologies** under the category of **Sovereign Health Intelligence** (الذكاء الصحي السيادي). Its core innovation is the **Isnād** audit architecture — a cryptographic, SHA-256 hash-chained record of every AI decision that captures WHO, WHAT, WHEN, and WHY. Tampering with any record breaks the chain and is mathematically provable.

## Seven Capability Pillars

| Pillar | What It Does |
|---|---|
| **Clinical Decision Engine** | Computes real-time risk scores (0–100), urgency classification, explainable contributing factors, and personalized recommendations for every patient encounter. |
| **Drug-Interaction Engine** | Severity-graded interaction detection (critical / high / moderate / low) at both prescribing and dispensing points, across all active medications. |
| **Digital Twin Projection** | Twelve-month health trajectory modeling per patient, identifying intervention windows and projected outcomes. |
| **Isnād Audit Chain** | Tamper-evident, hash-chained audit trail for every decision, access event, and consent change. Integrity verifiable in a single operation. |
| **Consent Enforcement** | Citizen-controlled data sharing with instant effect — revoking access locks out the affected party at the data boundary, not after a request cycle. |
| **Fraud & Anomaly Detection** | Behavioral pattern analysis for insurance claims, distinguishing clinical severity from billing anomalies. |
| **Twelve Role-Based Portals** | Physician, Citizen, Emergency, Ministry, AI Control, Lab, Pharmacy, Hospital, Insurance, Research, Family, Supply Chain — each role-appropriate and access-bounded. |
