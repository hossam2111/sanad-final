## Slide 2 — The Problem

**Title:** The Decision Gap in Saudi Healthcare

**Visual:** Three icons representing: Fragmented Data, Untraceable AI, Sovereignty Exposure.

**Speaker notes:** Three structural gaps persist in our health system. First, clinical data is fragmented — the prescribing system and the dispensing system don't talk. Second, as AI enters clinical workflows, no one can trace who made a decision, what data informed it, or whether the record has been altered. Third, reliance on foreign health-AI platforms creates dependencies inconsistent with PDPL and national sovereignty. SANAD addresses all three.

---

## Slide 3 — What SANAD Is

**Title:** Sovereign Health Intelligence — الذكاء الصحي السيادي

**Visual:** Single diagram: 12 portals surrounding a central Isnād audit chain.

**Speaker notes:** SANAD is a national AI health-intelligence platform built on one principle: every decision must be traceable, explainable, and sovereign. Twelve role-based portals serve every actor in the health ecosystem — physician, citizen, emergency responder, ministry official, pharmacist, insurer, researcher, family member, hospital administrator, lab technician, supply-chain operator, and an AI control center. At the center sits the Isnād — a cryptographic audit chain that records WHO, WHAT, WHEN, and WHY for every decision.

## Slide 4 — The Isnād Concept

**Title:** Isnād — A Chain of Trust

**Visual:** Side-by-side: classical Isnād chain (scholar transmission) and SANAD's SHA-256 hash chain.

**Speaker notes:** In our scholarly tradition, Isnād is the unbroken chain of transmission that establishes authenticity. In SANAD, every AI decision is hash-chained using SHA-256. If any record is altered, the chain breaks — and the tampering is mathematically provable. This is not a feature we added. It is the structural principle of the system. You will see it verified live in a few minutes.
